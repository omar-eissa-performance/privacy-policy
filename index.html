Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Omar Eissa ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our applications and services that connect to third-party advertising platforms including Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok, and Snapchat.

1. Information We Collect

Our applications access advertising account data on your behalf, including:

  • Ad campaign performance metrics (impressions, clicks, spend, conversions)
  • Ad creative assets (images, videos, ad copy)
  • Audience and targeting configurations
  • Account and business page identifiers

We do not collect personal data from end users who view or interact with your ads.

2. How We Use Information

We use the data accessed through these platforms solely to:

  • Generate performance reports for your advertising accounts
  • Analyze campaign effectiveness and provide optimization recommendations
  • Manage and create advertising campaigns on your behalf

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your advertising data with any third parties. Data is only accessible to you and our team for the purpose of managing your advertising accounts.

4. Data Storage and Security

Advertising data is processed locally and is not stored on external servers. API access tokens are stored securely and are never shared. We use industry-standard security practices to protect all credentials and data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain advertising performance data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email address below. Upon request, we will delete all stored data associated with your accounts within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any data we hold about your accounts
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke our application's access at any time through the respective platform's settings

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
